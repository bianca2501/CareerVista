Vision Statement

"To empower every student to discover their true potential and navigate their career path with confidence, clarity, and support, leading to a fulfilling and successful future."

Mission Statement

"To provide comprehensive and personalized career counseling services that guide students through critical educational milestones. By offering aptitude tests, expert counseling sessions, mental health support, and motivational resources, we aim to help students make informed decisions about their academic and career paths."

Our Story

Career Vista was founded by three third-year Engineering students and counselors who saw a need for personalized career guidance. We are dedicated to helping students discover their potential and make confident career choices.
