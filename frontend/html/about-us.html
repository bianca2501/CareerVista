About DigiLift Careers

Many students struggle with choosing the right career due to a lack of proper guidance and awareness. Existing career counseling services are often expensive or provide generic advice that does not meet individual needs. DigiLift Careers aims to address this issue by developing a web-based platform that offers AI-powered career recommendations.

Our project will integrate an AI chatbot to assist users with queries, schedule expert consultations, and analyze aptitude test results. Additionally, we plan to incorporate DigiLocker to access academic records for more data-driven suggestions. The platform will be built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Node.js, and MongoDB, ensuring a user-friendly experience.

While our project is in the initial stages, we aim to create a structured and accessible career guidance system. Some challenges we anticipate include ensuring data privacy, improving AI accuracy, and making the platform engaging for users. The project will require continuous development and testing to be truly effective. If successful, we hope to refine and expand it further in the future to provide more reliable and personalized career counseling.
