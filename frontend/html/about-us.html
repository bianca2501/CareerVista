About Our Project

Many students struggle with career decisions due to limited guidance, while existing counseling services are often expensive or generic. DigiLift Careers is an AI-powered web platform designed to provide personalized career recommendations through an interactive and data-driven approach.

The system integrates an AI chatbot for answering queries, expert consultations for in-depth guidance, and an aptitude test analysis module to assess students' strengths. Academic records are securely accessed via DigiLocker, ensuring seamless data integration.

The platform also features government scholarship integration, automatically matching students with relevant opportunities and providing real-time notifications for public sector job openings. Additionally, specialized career tracks, such as civil services preparation, offer mentorship from retired bureaucrats and "Policy Hackathons" to engage students in solving real-world governance challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI career recommendation work?

Our AI analyzes your aptitude test results, academic performance, interests, and skills to match you with the most suitable career options from our database of 60+ career paths. The system continuously learns and improves its recommendations based on user feedback and outcomes.

Is my academic data secure with DigiLocker integration?

Absolutely. We use secure APIs to access only the necessary academic records from DigiLocker with your explicit permission. Your data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. We comply with all data protection regulations to ensure your information remains private and secure.

How are the government scholarship matches determined?

Our system cross-references your academic profile, financial background (if provided), and career aspirations with the latest government scholarship databases. We match you with all eligible scholarships and provide application deadlines, requirements, and direct links to apply.

What makes your civil services preparation track special?

Our civil services track is developed with guidance from retired bureaucrats who understand the examination process intimately. Beyond standard preparation, we offer unique features like Policy Hackathons where you can work on real governance challenges, mentorship from former civil servants, and a comprehensive roadmap that adapts to your learning progress.
