🎓 Courses After 10th in Commerce Stream

📊 Core Commerce & Finance

  • B.Com. (Bachelor of Commerce) 📚 – Explore accounting, finance, and taxation.
  • B.Com. (Honours) 🧾 – Specialize in commerce with advanced knowledge.
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) 📈 – Learn business management and strategy.
  • BBI (Bachelor of Banking and Insurance) 🏦 – Dive into banking and insurance.
  • BFM (Bachelor of Financial Markets) 📊 – Explore stock markets and trading.
  • BAF (Bachelor of Accounting and Finance) 💹 – Master accounting and financial management.

📢 Management & Business Studies

  • BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies) 📋 – Develop leadership and business strategy skills.
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) 📈 – Focus on decision-making and organizational skills.
  • Integrated MBA (5-Year Program) 🏢 – Build a strong foundation in business management.

💻 IT & Technology in Commerce

  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) 💻 – Combine technology with business applications.
  • B.Sc. in Information Technology (IT) 🌐 – Explore IT solutions for commerce.
  • B.Sc. in Data Analytics 📊 – Analyze business data for decision-making.

📜 Economics & Policy Studies

  • BA in Economics 📉 – Study global financial trends and economies.
  • B.Sc. in Economics 📊 – Focus on quantitative analysis and economic models.

⚖️ Law & Legal Studies

  • BA LLB (Integrated Law Program) ⚖️ – Combine business and law.
  • BBA LLB (Bachelor of Business Administration & Law) 🏛️ – Focus on corporate law and legal business.

🏦 Banking, Finance & Insurance

  • B.Com. in Banking & Insurance 🏢 – Explore the world of banking and insurance.
  • B.Com. in Financial Markets 📈 – Learn about stock exchanges and trading.
  • B.Com. in Accounting & Finance 📊 – Master accounting and financial management.

🏡 Entrepreneurship & Family Business

  • BBA in Entrepreneurship 🚀 – Learn to start and manage your own business.
  • BMS in Family Business Management 🏭 – Manage and grow your family business.

🎨 Creative & Media Fields

  • B.Des. (Bachelor of Design) 🎨 – Explore creativity and visual arts.
  • BMM (Bachelor of Mass Media) 🎥 – Dive into media, advertising, and journalism.
  • BBA in Media Management 📹 – Manage media, PR, and content.

🎮 Gaming, Animation & Multimedia

  • B.Sc. in Animation & Multimedia 📹 – Combine creativity with technology.
  • B.Sc. in Game Design & Development 🎮 – Design virtual worlds and games.

✈️ Aviation & Logistics

  • BBA in Aviation Management ✈️ – Manage airline operations.
  • B.Sc. in Logistics & Supply Chain Management 🚚 – Manage supply chains and operations.

📈 Specialized & Emerging Fields

  • B.Sc. in Financial Analytics 📊 – Analyze data for predictive finance.
  • B.Com. in E-Commerce 🛒 – Explore digital business and online markets.
  • BBA in Digital Marketing 📢 – Master digital platforms and marketing strategies.

💡 Actuarial & Risk Management

  • B.Sc. in Actuarial Science 📉 – Explore statistics, probability, and risk assessment.
  • B.Com. in Risk Management 🔐 – Mitigate financial uncertainties.
