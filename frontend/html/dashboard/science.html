🎓 Courses After 10th in Science Stream

🔬 Medical & Allied Sciences

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) 🏥 – Become a doctor and save lives.
  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) 🦷 – Specialize in dental care and oral health.
  • BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery) 🌿 – Explore traditional Ayurvedic healing.
  • BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine & Surgery) 🏡 – Study natural and alternative medicine.
  • B.V.Sc (Bachelor of Veterinary Science) 🐾 – Care for animals and their health.
  • BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy) 🦵 – Help people recover physically.
  • B.Sc. Nursing 🩺 – Provide care and support as a nurse.

🧪 Pure Sciences & Research

  • B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, Biology 🔬 – Explore natural phenomena and experiments.
  • B.Sc. in Biotechnology 🧬 – Dive into genetic engineering and research.
  • B.Sc. in Microbiology 🦠 – Study microorganisms and their impact.
  • B.Sc. in Environmental Science 🌱 – Protect nature and study ecosystems.
  • B.Sc. in Agriculture 🌾 – Innovate in farming and food production.
  • B.Sc. in Forensic Science 🔍 – Solve mysteries and analyze crimes.

💻 Engineering & Technology

  • B.E./B.Tech. (Bachelor of Engineering / Technology) ⚙️ – Build and design systems.
  • Computer Science 💻 – Code and develop software.
  • Electronics and Communication 📡 – Work with gadgets and communication systems.
  • Mechanical Engineering 🔧 – Innovate with machines and tools.
  • Civil Engineering 🏗️ – Design infrastructure and construction projects.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science 🤖 – Explore AI and big data.

📚 Mathematics & Statistics

  • B.Sc. in Mathematics 📐 – Solve equations and abstract problems.
  • B.Sc. in Statistics 📊 – Analyze data and trends.
  • B.Sc. in Actuarial Science 📈 – Manage financial risks and probabilities.

✈️ Aviation & Maritime

  • B.Sc. in Aviation ✈️ – Work in the aviation industry.
  • B.Tech. in Aeronautical Engineering 🚀 – Design aircraft and spacecraft.
  • B.Sc. in Nautical Science ⚓ – Explore maritime careers.

🏢 Management & Business

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) 📈 – Learn business strategy and leadership.
  • BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies) 📊 – Manage organizations and people.
  • Integrated MBA (5-Year Course) 🏢 – Build a strong foundation in management.

🎮 Design, Media & Creative Arts

  • B.Des. (Bachelor of Design) 🎨 – Explore creativity and design.
  • B.Sc. in Animation & Multimedia 📹 – Create digital art and storytelling.
  • B.Arch. (Bachelor of Architecture) 🏛️ – Design buildings and urban spaces.

📡 IT & Computer Applications

  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) 💻 – Develop software and applications.
  • B.Sc. in Information Technology 🌐 – Manage IT solutions.
  • B.Sc. in Data Science 📊 – Analyze data and AI.
  • B.Sc. in Cybersecurity 🔒 – Protect systems and networks.

⚖️ Law & Legal Studies

  • BA LLB (Integrated Law Course) ⚖️ – Advocate for justice and law.
