🎓 Courses After 10th in Arts Stream

📚 Core Humanities & Social Sciences

  • BA in History 🏺 – Explore ancient civilizations and historical events.
  • BA in Political Science 🏛️ – Study politics, governance, and policymaking.
  • BA in Sociology 👥 – Understand social behavior and cultural norms.
  • BA in Psychology 🧠 – Dive into the human mind and behavior.
  • BA in Philosophy 🤔 – Explore fundamental questions about existence and knowledge.

🎨 Fine Arts, Design & Performing Arts

  • BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) 🎨 – Express creativity through painting, sculpture, and visual arts.
  • B.Des. (Bachelor of Design) ✨ – Explore graphic, fashion, or interior design.
  • BA in Performing Arts 🎭 – Pursue acting, music, or dance.
  • BPA (Bachelor of Performing Arts) 🎤 – Enhance your artistic skills with hands-on exposure.

📝 Media, Communication & Journalism

  • BA in Mass Communication & Journalism 📺 – Explore storytelling, reporting, and media.
  • BMM (Bachelor of Mass Media) 🎥 – Dive into advertising, journalism, and public relations.
  • BA in Film Studies 🎬 – Study filmmaking and cinema analysis.

📢 Advertising, Public Relations & Event Management

  • BA in Advertising & Public Relations 📢 – Explore PR, branding, and media communication.
  • BBA in Event Management 🎉 – Learn to manage large-scale events.

💼 Law, Policy & International Relations

  • BA LLB (Integrated Law Program) ⚖️ – Combine law with humanities.
  • BBA LLB (Bachelor of Business Administration & Law) 🏢 – Focus on business and corporate law.
  • BA in International Relations 🌍 – Study global politics and diplomacy.

💡 Psychology, Counseling & Social Work

  • BA in Psychology 🧠 – Understand human behavior and emotions.
  • BSW (Bachelor of Social Work) 🌱 – Create social change and make a difference.
  • BA in Human Development 👩‍👧 – Study human growth and social development.

🎭 Literature, Language & Linguistics

  • BA in English Literature 📚 – Analyze literature and explore cultures.
  • BA in Linguistics 🔤 – Study how languages work.
  • BA in French/German/Spanish 🇫🇷 🇩🇪 🇪🇸 – Learn foreign languages and explore cultures.

✈️ Travel, Tourism & Hospitality

  • BA in Travel & Tourism Management ✈️ – Manage tours and explore tourism careers.
  • BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management) 🏨 – Manage hotels, resorts, and hospitality services.

🎮 Animation, Multimedia & Game Design

  • B.Sc. in Animation & Multimedia 🎥 – Create digital content, animation, and gaming.
  • B.Des. in Game Design 🎮 – Develop virtual realities and interactive experiences.

🎓 Education, Teaching & Training

  • BA in Education 🏫 – Shape future minds through teaching.
  • B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) 📚 – Become a professional teacher.

🌏 Anthropology, Geography & Environmental Studies

  • BA in Anthropology 🌱 – Study human evolution and cultural diversity.
  • BA in Geography 🌍 – Explore physical landscapes and geographical phenomena.
  • BA in Environmental Studies 🌿 – Focus on environmental protection and sustainability.

📈 Management & Business Studies

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) 📊 – Learn business strategy and leadership.
  • BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies) 🏢 – Focus on business administration and marketing.

✨ Specialized & Emerging Fields

  • BA in Gender Studies 👩‍🎓 – Understand gender, power, and culture.
  • BA in Cultural Studies 🎭 – Analyze diverse cultures and societies.
  • BA in Criminology 🔍 – Explore the criminal justice system and law enforcement.
